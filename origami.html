What is Origami?

Origami is the art of folding paper to construct models of your liking.Orgami originated from China and Akira Yoshizawa sparked the national interest in the art of Origami

Examples and Types of Origami folding

  • Valley/ Mountain folding
  • Pleat fold
  • Outside Reverse Fold
  • Crimp Fold
  • Rabbit Ear
  • Inside Reverse Fold
  • Squash
  • Petal Fold

Lets make a Origami Car!

  1. Start with a square sheet of origami paper and fold paper in half from top to bottom, then unfold. Now fold paper in half again left to right and unfold
  2. Fold bottom edge to center
  3. Diagonally fold down the right and left sides of the bottom flap to create the wheels
  4. Fold down the top at horizontal crease, covering the wheels
  5. Next fold 3/4 of it back up
  6. Fold down top right corner
  7. Turn figure over and view your final product! Don't forget to add your windows!
